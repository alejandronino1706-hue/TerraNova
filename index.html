Introduction

TerraNova is a team made up of students who share the goal of improving quality of life and proposing a sustainable plan for the expansion of cities without affecting the environment in the process.

This project bases its actions on geoscientific data, which helps us gain a better perspective when executing our plan in different areas of the world.
